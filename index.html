Short Biography I am an assistant professor in the Department of Computer Science & Technology, University of Science and Technology Beijing. Currently, I also work as a "Macao Young Scholars Program" postdoctor advised by Prof. Zhiguo Gong. I received my PhD degree from Institute of Information System & Engineering, School of Software, Tsinghua University, where I was co-advised by Prof. Xiaojun Ye and Prof. Chaokun Wang. Before that, I obtained my bachelor degree and master degree from Beijing JiaoTong University, advised by Prof. Yao Zhao.

Research Interests

1) Social network: network embedding, information diffusion.

2) Artificial intelligence: statistical machine learning, deep learning, causality.

Research Project

Zero-shot Graph Embedding (ZGE) refers to the process of learning discriminative graph embeddings when labeled data cannot cover all classes.
