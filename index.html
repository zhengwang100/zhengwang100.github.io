Short Biography

I am an Associate Professor and PhD Advisor at Shanghai Jiao Tong University. I received my PhD from Tsinghua University, following my Bachelor's and Master's degrees from Beijing Jiaotong University. My research focuses on data mining, deep learning and big data. I am equally interested in designing algorithms and systems with strong theoretical foundations and practical implementations for real-world use. Some current projects include:

  • rLLM (relationLLM), an easy-to-use Pytorch library for Relational Table Learning with LLMs.
