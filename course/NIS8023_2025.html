Special Topic on Table Learning / 表格学习专题

Course Information (Spring 2025) / 课程信息

English: This is a Spring 2025 special series course on "Table Learning". It will cover the classical and state-of-the-art table learning methods and algorithms. This course offers the necessary background for students who would like to pursue research in the area of data mining and machine learning or continue to take a related job in these areas. This content is part of the graduate course "Social Network Analysis (NIS8023)" at Shanghai Jiao Tong University.

中文： 这个是一个2025年春季学期，关于“表格学习”的专题系列课程。课程将涵盖经典与前沿的表格学习方法和算法。该课程为有意从事数据挖掘和机器学习研究或在相关领域就业的学生提供必要的背景知识。这部分内容是上海交通大学2025年春季学期研究生课程 “Social Network Analysis (NIS8023)” 的一部分。
