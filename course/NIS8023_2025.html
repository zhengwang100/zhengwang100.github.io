课程信息/Course Information

本课程是一个关于"表格学习"的专题系列课程，涵盖经典和最先进的表格学习方法与算法。该课程为希望在数据挖掘和机器学习领域继续研究或从事相关工作的学生提供必要的背景知识。

This course offers a special topic series on table learning, covering classical and state-of-the-art table learning methods and algorithms. It provides essential background for students pursuing research or careers in data mining and machine learning.
