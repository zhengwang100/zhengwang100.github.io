Instruction to Applicants

Students

Data is the center of my research. Data is also the basic of IT society. There are two research groups in my lab.

1) Data mining: social network analysis, graph nerual networks, and deep learning.

2) Database: blockchain related data architectures, and large-scale transaction processing systems.

In data mining research, we design new theories for graph data related deep learning. Therefore, if you are very like mathematical theories (like matrix analysis and probability statistics), you are very encouraged to join the data mining group. In database research, we not only design new algorithms but also write new codes for database systems. Therefore, if you are very like data structures and algorithms (like B-tree and dynamic programming), you are very encouraged to join the database group.

Reminder: The prospective students are highly encouraged to join (either offline or online) my group meeting for at least one time to get a better understanding about my group. This will help you make good decisions based on transparency.
