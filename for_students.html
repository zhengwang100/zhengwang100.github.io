Instruction to Applicants

Students

Data is at the center of many challenges in computer science today. We aim to create meaningful, simple, and robust data management systems/tools. If you are also very interested in this topic, you are very welcome to join my lab in one of the following two research groups.

1) Data mining: social network analysis, graph neural networks, and deep learning. In this direction, we design new theories for graph data related deep learning, following the way of the famous Prof. Judea Pearl. Therefore, if you are very like mathematical theories (like matrix analysis and probability statistics), you are very encouraged to join the data mining group.

2) Database: blockchain related data architectures, and large-scale transaction processing systems. In this direction, we not only design new algorithms but also write new codes for database systems, following the way of the famous Prof. Michael Stonebraker. Therefore, if you are very like data structures and algorithms (like B-tree and dynamic programming), you are very encouraged to join the database group.

Reminder: The prospective students are highly encouraged to join (either offline or online) my group meeting for at least one time to get a better understanding about my group. This will help you make good decisions based on transparency.
